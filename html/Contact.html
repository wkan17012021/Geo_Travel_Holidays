Reach Out to Us

The Road Less Travelled was created to share opinions and suggestions for those searching for something different from what traditional travel agencies typically offer. We want to make sure that your trip is tailored for you. We can offer travel and logistics guidance, book flights, cost-cutting tips or even plan your full itinerary so you'll feel more comfortable knowing everything is being taken care of. Please send us a message, email or give our team a call to book flights. If you prefer, we can arrange an in-person appointment. Our assistance doesn't end when you take off either; our team are on hand to assist with any issues you may have.

Questions, suggestions and feedback are all welcome!

If you would like to keep up to date about new geological discoveries on holiday, please sign up for our newsletter!
