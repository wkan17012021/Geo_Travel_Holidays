Explore the Great Unknown

The Road Less Travelled offers tour-led trips or tailored itineraries to sites with a particular geological interest. You can rest at ease knowing your holiday is in safe hands of one of our experienced and qualified instructors. But, if you prefer a more solitary adventure, we also offer trips packed full of must-see sights, discount rates and exclusive access allowing you the flexibility to explore at your own pace. So, whether you are seeking a relaxing but structured holiday as a group or prefer your indepedence on your next expedition, The Road Less Travelled has something to offer everyone. No geological knowledge is required beforehand, as our tour guides will present the geological history on site and will be illustrated in our field handbooks and guides provided to you. Head over to the contact page for more details.

Current Tours on Offer

Experience the Alps at a relaxing pace aboard the Golden Pass Railway Line. With The Road Less Travelled, you will get discounted tickets on this Scenic Route through Switzerland. Additionally, we will arrange nearby accomodation in Geneva for a weekend of shopping and sightseeing in the capital as well as onward travel to Luzern where your rail journey begins.

What amazes us about the US, is the sheer size of the country. Each state is truly disimilar to the next, both in culture and in landscape. For an epic geological adventure, try our 6-states in 10 days coach tour starting out from the sun-shine state, all the way up the mid-west and north to Yellow Stone National Park. Whatever your interest, the States will not dissapoint!

For more details about star attractions, take a sneak peek at our Top Spots Page.

The Dolomite Mountains is a UNESCO World Heritage Site with a lot to offer the inquisitive geological wonderer. Our tour to northern Italy will take you hiking through the mountains and our guide will explain how this land comprised of ocean coral reefs deposited around 250 million years ago, has been transformed into the mountain range today. During the visit you will be staying in the romantic city of Venice.

Some may picture China as sprawling conurbations but it certainly has some unique and remote spots of geological splendour. Get away from the metropolis and start exporing the wilderness of western China! With The Road Less Travelled, we will sort out air travel from your city of stay in-country, to Zhangye Airport, Gansu Province and onwards with coach travel to the local geopark with hotel stay.

Take a look at our Zhangye section in our Top Spots Page for more details.

If warmer weather takes your fancy, our Desert Tour will take you away from the hustle and bustle of Dubai and into the 'outback' of the Middle East. You will get the opportunity to traverse the desert via camel, sandboard or 4x4. Meals and refreshments are complimentary. Although this package tour comes at a premium, rest assured it will leave you with exhilirating memories of the Middle East.

Iceland is a must: travellers will be one with nature on our golden circle tour. Don't forget to bring your thermals!

We will arrange accomodation in Reykjavik, with visits to Thingvellir National Park, Geysir and Gullfoss. Please see the Top Spots Page for our favourite place to visit in Iceland.
